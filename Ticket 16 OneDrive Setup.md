<h1>Ticket 16: OneDrive Setup</h1>

<h2>Description</h2>
This lab example shows how to setup Microsoft Office 365 OneDrive for a user.

<h2>Environments Used </h2>

- <b>MacBook Pro (Intel)</b>
- <b>VMware Fusion</b>
- <b>Microsoft 365</b>
- <b>OneDrive</b>
